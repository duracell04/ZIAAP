## Classification rules

- Maturity labels describe demonstrated evidence, not visual polish or ambition.
- “High fidelity” at C0 and C1 means workflow and interaction fidelity only.
- Internal conformance, expert review, field validation, and production evidence
  must remain distinct.
- A later label cannot be used until every required gate has recorded evidence.
- All estimates after the currently authorised stage are provisional.
- Partner ownership and the earlier dispute-value range are superseded as
  current strategy. Economic benefits remain unestablished until their
  specific market and outcome evidence is approved.
- The 70-week total is an arithmetic planning horizon, not an upfront delivery
  or funding commitment.

## Mandatory decision gates

### C1 — after Sprint 4

Audit concept comprehension, contract/compiler integrity, artifact eligibility,
truthful provenance, known limitations, and the funding roadmap. Collect real
reviewer and commercial evidence, re-estimate Sprints 5–34, then record **go,
refine, narrow, or hold**.

### P1 — after Sprint 9

Audit persistent bilateral participation, unseen-document handling, live
divergence detection, initial measurable value, architecture, and user evidence.
Re-estimate Sprints 10–34, then record **go, refine, narrow, or hold**.
