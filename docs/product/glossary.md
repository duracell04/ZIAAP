## Canonical authority statement

> Models may propose scenarios, representations and analysis; they do not
> control authoritative state, reveal sealed responses, overwrite party text,
> commit consequential transitions or exercise legal authority. Parties control
> their own positions and bilateral dispositions. Applicable law and valid
> external acts govern contractual or procedural effect, and any later neutral
> retains independent authority and responsibility.

## Governing claims principle

> **Sell the narrow product. Preserve the larger architecture. Prove the bridge
> between them.**
