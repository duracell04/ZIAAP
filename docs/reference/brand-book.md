# ZIAAP Brand Book

> Current normative brand reference. The
> [Pilot 01 Protocol](../product/pilot-01-protocol.md), product charter,
> glossary and claims register control current product, maturity and authority
> wording. The [operating model](../product/operating-model.md) controls the
> deferred I0-I2 architecture.

## 1. Brand hierarchy

Category:

> **Computational Private Ordering**

Current product:

> **ZIAAP Contract Alignment**

Product promise:

> **Align material contract expectations before you sign.**

Canonical description:

> **ZIAAP is a contract-alignment system for complex commercial agreements. It
> converts selected clauses into concrete operational scenarios, collects the
> parties' independent expectations, identifies materially different outcomes,
> and produces a versioned record of what was aligned, deliberately left open,
> or incorporated into the contract.**

The current commercial wedge is limited to complex enterprise
systems-integration MSAs and their associated documents. Lifecycle
re-alignment, segregated mediation and structured export to independent
professionals or institutions are future extensions.

The strategic principle may appear on strategy surfaces:

> **Sell the narrow product. Preserve the larger architecture. Prove the bridge
> between them.**

## 2. Current boundary

Describe the current artifact as a **C0 high-fidelity interactive concept
demonstrator**, immediately qualifying high fidelity as workflow and
interaction fidelity. It is synthetic, resettable, simulation-only and without
legal effect. Pilot 01 is a specification, not an active pilot or market claim.

ZIAAP OpCo owns software and product revenue but has no neutral authority.
Alignment facilitators may operate I0, but they may not mediate or adjudicate a
dispute arising from that engagement. Any future mediator, arbitrator,
professional or institution retains its own authority under a separate
engagement or appointment.

The former two-wedge comparison, partner-owned institution, CHF
50,000-500,000 dispute target and category-first positioning are superseded as
current strategy. They may appear only as dated history or qualified future
hypotheses.

The AI Resolution Officer is deferred governed software, never a legal office,
institution, arbitrator or autonomous decision-maker. A properly appointed
human arbitrator retains legal authority and responsibility.

## 3. Voice

Precise, calm, institutional, candid and commercially literate. State future
architecture in intentional language--"may," "future," "deferred"--and state
current evidence conservatively.

Prefer align, compare, confirm, correct, reveal, resolve, preserve, defer,
incorporate, version, inspect and record.

Do not use validated, appointed, signed, award, binding, secure, autonomous,
enforceable, first, faster, scalable or economically viable as a current
positive claim. Do not describe the product as AI arbitration or promise that
a record has guaranteed evidentiary or legal effect.

## 5. Visual character

Warm institutional paper, charcoal structure, oxblood authority cautions,
reasoning blue, evidence green and amber uncertainty. Serif display typography
signals deliberation; restrained sans-serif and monospace identify controls,
status, versions and digests.

Use aviation only for small I0/I1/I2 context labels in long-term architecture.
Do not introduce cockpit dashboards, aircraft controls or aviation-themed
actions into the current product story.

The interface should feel like a governed professional instrument, not a
generic chatbot, court portal, crypto product or futuristic control room.

## 6. Semantic treatments

Original party text is preserved separately from AI-normalised text. Party
assertions identify the party. AI inferences are visually distinct,
source-linked and uncertain. A party must confirm or correct its representation
before symmetric comparison. Legal effect, confidentiality, permitted use and
record kind are separate fields rather than one status label.

An AI inference must never resemble an established fact. A non-binding record
must never resemble an incorporated contract term. A reasoning memorandum must
never resemble a verdict.

## 7. Required artifact fields

Every consequential alignment artifact shows actor, acting capacity, authority
evidence, original text, derived text, confirmation disposition, record kind,
legal effect, permitted use, confidentiality or privilege claim, governing
version, effective period, approvals, provenance and supersession. C0 artifacts
also show their synthetic limitations.

## 8. Interaction language

Approved current-product labels include:

- `Begin guided demonstration`
- `Select clauses`
- `Approve scenarios`
- `Seal responses`
- `Confirm or correct representation`
- `Reveal material divergence`
- `Resolve, consciously leave open, or defer`
- `Record external adoption evidence`
- `Lock version`
- `Restart`

Expert-reference labels for deferred architecture remain available only inside
clearly marked future-extension simulations. Failures name cause and recovery.
Never silently substitute an illustrative fixture after live failure.

## 9. Opening and responsive composition

The opening leads with Contract Alignment, the systems-integration MSA scope,
current C0 classification and the party/software authority split. I0-I2 and
six gates are presented as preserved long-term architecture, not a bundle
currently for sale.

The public opening keeps one primary action. Market statistics, institutional
detail and deferred dispute workflows stay outside the current-product golden
path.

Desktop uses editorial asymmetry and generous whitespace. Mobile stacks roles,
metadata, profiles, positions, options and gates without losing labels. Print
suppresses navigation and actions while preserving artifact boundaries.

## 10. Brand acceptance

Release is blocked if a viewer confuses:

- the Pilot 01 specification with executed validation;
- future architecture with current C0 software;
- curated simulation with live execution;
- model normalisation with party-authored text;
- a non-binding record with an interpretive annex or incorporated term;
- ZIAAP OpCo or a facilitator with a neutral decision-maker;
- future mediation or adjudication with the current product; or
- a superseded partner-ownership, pricing or category-first hypothesis with a
  present product or company fact.
