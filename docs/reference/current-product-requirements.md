# Current Concept Product Requirements

> Normative requirements for the current C0 implementation. The
> [Pilot 01 Protocol](../product/pilot-01-protocol.md) controls the current
> product and proposed validation design. The
> [operating model](../product/operating-model.md) controls deferred I0-I2
> terminology.

## 1. Objective and boundary

Enable commercial lawyers, systems-integration transaction teams, design
partners and investors to understand **ZIAAP Contract Alignment** without
mistaking:

- simulation for authority or executed validation;
- AI-normalised text for a party-authored position;
- a non-binding record for an interpretive annex or incorporated term;
- OpCo or an alignment facilitator for a neutral decision-maker;
- a future mediation or adjudication workspace for the current product; or
- superseded partner-ownership, pricing and category-first hypotheses for
  current company facts.

The repository remains a high-fidelity interactive concept demonstrator, where
high fidelity is limited to workflow and interaction fidelity. It contains no
confidential contracts, pilot participants or legally effective actions.

## 2. Current product and Pilot 01

The current product is a scenario-based pre-signature alignment workflow for
complex enterprise systems-integration MSAs. It selects three to five clauses,
develops five to ten economically consequential scenarios, collects and
confirms sealed independent responses, reveals material divergence
symmetrically and produces a versioned, legally typed record.

Every meaningful state transition requires an attributable human act. Model
output is always a proposal. It cannot reveal responses, change authoritative
state, assign legal status or lock a version.

All Pilot 01 outputs default to non-binding. An interpretive annex or
incorporated term requires a separate external adoption act by authorised
parties. ZIAAP may record evidence of that act but does not create legal effect.

## 3. Preserved expert experience: three layers and six gates

The `/reference` experience remains a synthetic view of the long-term
architecture:

1. **I0 - Alignment** compares independent expectations and confirms exact
   language.
2. **I0 - Configuration** combines the Constitution control plane and scenario
   testing.
3. **I1 - Appointment & Configuration Freeze** prepares the exact manifest and
   contains an explicitly fictional appointment interaction.
4. **I1 - Case Production** presents claims, defences, facts, evidence, missing
   evidence, calculations and objections as structured state.
5. **I2 - Independent Adjudication** records a preliminary fictional human view
   before revealing the advisory reasoning memorandum and exposes adopt,
   modify, reject and request-more-evidence controls.
6. **I2 - Simulated Outcome & Procedural Black Box** presents the event-derived
   record and existing artifacts without calling the result an award.

Configuration manifest remains a Gate 3 technical artifact, not a lifecycle
stage. The reasoning memorandum is a Gate 5 advisory artifact, not the
authoritative case model. Gates 3-6 are future extensions, not the current
commercial product.

## 4. Public experience

The root redirects to `/demo`, which remains a deterministic five-step
projection:

|---|---|---|
| Align | Gate 1 | Current product |
| Test scenarios | Gate 2 | Current product |

| Human review | Gate 5 | Future extension |

Gate 3 is not implied to be part of the current public product. The legal
appointment interaction is unavailable in the public simulation. Public
routes, the `MinimalDemoState` v1 schema, golden case, source drawer,
one-action progression, session persistence, Back/Forward behavior and offline
execution remain stable.

## 5. State requirements

- Existing `ContractState` lifecycle statuses remain operational-readiness
  state for the preserved demonstrator.
- `getActiveMatterGate` and `getGateReadiness` remain pure projections.
- `buildStructuredCaseState` is the primary deferred case projection.
- `buildReasoningMemorandumInput` excludes sealed settlement content.
- `buildProceduralBlackBox` is generated from recorded events and excludes
  private fictional deliberation text.
- Behavior-affecting configuration changes invalidate manifests, dispute
  binding and downstream reasoning artifacts.
- Any future Pilot 01 implementation must preserve original and normalised text
  as separate immutable objects and use the legal-status fields in the Pilot
  01 Protocol.

## 6. Authority requirements

- Enforce `simulation_only` and `legalEffect: false` in the current artifact.
- Keep execution, lifecycle status, record kind and legal effect separate.
- Make `authoritative`, `validated` and ordinary appointed state unreachable.
- Label actor, status, version, consequence, provenance and limitation on
  consequential artifacts.
- ZIAAP OpCo has no neutral authority.
- Alignment facilitators may operate I0 but may not mediate or adjudicate a
  dispute arising from that engagement.
- A properly appointed human arbitrator retains judgment and any future
  signature.

## 7. Execution and failure requirements

Expert reference routes may expose distinct curated-simulation and live
actions. Fixtures return `illustrative_only`; successful declared live calls
return `executed_unverified`; every live error returns `failed` with a reason
and no fallback success.

The public walkthrough makes zero model or application-API calls. Its optional
feedback form is a disclosed exception to offline operation: the browser posts
the required-email research response directly to the temporary FormSubmit
processor. It does not add an application backend.

## 8. Accessibility and responsive requirements

Keyboard focus is visible; semantic controls and status regions are used;
colour is never the sole status cue; desktop and mobile have no page-level
horizontal scroll; and dense detail uses progressive disclosure. Required
browser widths include desktop, laptop, tablet and 390 px.

## 9. Claims requirements

Public and investor surfaces may state the category **Computational Private
Ordering**, the current product **ZIAAP Contract Alignment**, its current scope
and its C0 boundary. Lifecycle re-alignment, segregated mediation and
structured export are future extensions.

Partner ownership, arbitration transformation, CHF 50,000-500,000 dispute
targeting and "first" positioning are superseded as active claims. External
sources establish context or precedent only. They do not validate ZIAAP,
guarantee legal or evidentiary effect, establish AI Act classification or prove
enforceability.

## 10. Acceptance

The branch requires typecheck, lint, tests, production build,
`git diff --check`, complete public critical paths, responsive browser checks
and no console errors.

The 2026-07-24 decision approves product direction only. Legal-framing,
privacy, security, market, usability and production approvals and evidence
remain pending. Publication does not satisfy a Pilot 01 gate.

## 11. Non-goals

No product feature, pilot execution, recruitment, pricing experiment,
application backend, authentication, confidential data, real appointment,
corporate ownership implementation, signature, award, production case
management, independent validation, sovereign runtime, Council of LLMs, DLT
or enforcement is included.
