## Hyper-minimal public walkthrough addendum

Use this section only for `/demo`.

1. In one sentence, what do you understand ZIAAP to do now?
2. Which walkthrough steps are the current product?
3. Which steps are future architecture?
4. Who confirmed the scenario representations?
5. What could the parties challenge or correct?
6. Who could incorporate any wording into a contract?
7. What concrete business value, if any, did you perceive?
8. What would prevent your organisation from trying this process?
9. Where could it potentially save the greatest time, cost or uncertainty?

Any belief that the walkthrough accepts a real contract, appoints a neutral,
issues an operative decision, executes live AI, validates legal conclusions,
creates legal effect or demonstrates cost/time savings is recorded as an
authority or evidence-category error.
